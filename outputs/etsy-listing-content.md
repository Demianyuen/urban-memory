### Product Description

#### Overview

**Your city's building footprints, delivered as clean vector files.**

This is a digital download of custom city map data in industry-standard vector formats. Every building footprint in your chosen area is included as a closed polyline, ready for laser cutting, CNC routing, 3D modeling, screen printing, or any other production workflow.

Data sourced from OpenStreetMap and processed through our proprietary pipeline. Each file is hand-validated to ensure clean geometry -- no overlapping paths, no stray vertices, no missing data.

Choose your format bundle:

**SVG Only ($19)**

**DXF Bundle ($29)**

**Full Bundle: SVG + DXF + DWG ($39)**
