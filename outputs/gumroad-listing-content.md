### File Formats & Specs

| Format | Version | Units | Geometry | File Size (typical) |
|--------|---------|-------|----------|---------------------|
| SVG | 1.1 | ViewBox | `<path>` elements | 200 KB - 2 MB |
| DXF | AutoCAD R2010 | Millimeters | LWPOLYLINE, closed | 500 KB - 5 MB |
| DWG | AutoCAD R2004 | Millimeters | LWPOLYLINE, closed | 500 KB - 5 MB |

**Coordinate System**
- SVG: ViewBox aligned to city bounding box
- DXF/DWG: UTM projected, centered on origin (0,0) for CNC/laser bed workflows

**Precision**
- All geometry output to 4 decimal places
- No overlapping paths, no duplicate vertices
- Validated against OSM data integrity checks

### 6 Style Examples

Each style transforms the same raw building data into a distinct visual character:

**BLUEPRINT**
The classic. Deep navy water and background, luminous ice-blue buildings. Inspired by 19th-century cyanotype photography and architectural blueprints. The most architectural of the six styles.
- Background: #0a2a5a | Buildings: #8eb9ff
- Best for: Architects, engineers, technical decor

**CHARCOAL**
Maximum contrast. Near-black background, silver-white buildings. No color distraction -- just pure urban form. This is the minimalist's choice.
- Background: #1a1a1a | Buildings: #e0e0e0
- Best for: Monochrome interiors, offices, modern galleries

**URBAN_GRAIN**
Warm and expressive. Cream background with randomized multi-color buildings in blues, reds, oranges, teals, and ambers. Each print is unique -- the color assignment changes per generation.
- Background: #FDF3E3 | Palette: 5 colors assigned randomly
- Best for: Creative spaces, gifts, colorful interiors

**NOLLI**
The architecture historian's favorite. Pure figure-ground representation: white building masses on a dark midnight field. Named after Giambattista Nolli's 1748 map of Rome.
- Background: #1a1a2e | Buildings: #f8f9fa
- Best for: Architecture lovers, scholars, dramatic wall art

**TERRA**
Mediterranean warmth. A curated palette of terracotta, sienna, ochre, umber, and sandstone tones on a parchment background. Feels like an archaeological find.
- Background: #e8ddd0 | Palette: 5 earth tones
- Best for: Warm interiors, rustic decor, natural aesthetics

**SEPIA**
Vintage photograph aesthetic. Soft warm monotone with brown buildings on aged paper. Timeless and elegant, like a city remembered from another era.
- Background: #f5ecd7 | Buildings: #8B7D6B
- Best for: Traditional decor, nostalgic gifts, vintage themes

### How It Works

**Step 1: Purchase**
Select your tier (SVG, DXF, or Full Bundle) and complete checkout.

**Step 2: Tell Us Your City**
After purchase, you will receive a Gumroad welcome email with a Google Form link. Fill in:
- City name and area (e.g., "Sheung Wan, Hong Kong")
- Style preference
- Map radius (default: 1200m)
- Custom text for the poster (title, subtitle, optional coordinates)

**Step 3: We Generate**
Our pipeline fetches real OpenStreetMap building footprint data for your exact location, processes it through our validation engine, and renders it in your chosen style and format.

**Step 4: Download**
Within 24 hours, your custom ZIP file is uploaded to Gumroad and available in your Library. You will also receive a download link via email.
